# Style and Conventions
- Lint baseline: `analysis_options.yaml` includes `package:flutter_lints/flutter.yaml`.
- Analyzer excludes: `submodules/**`.
- Naming follows Dart/Flutter norms seen in repo:
  - Files: `snake_case`
  - Classes/types: `PascalCase`
  - Variables/functions/methods: `camelCase`
- Codebase is structured by concern (pages/widgets/layouts/providers/models/database/utils).
- Keep code Flutter/Dart idiomatic and compatible with Flutter lints.
